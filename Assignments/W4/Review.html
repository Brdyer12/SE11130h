REVIEW

Ben Dyer
May 20, 2025

Borat is a hilarious satirical film that pushes the boundaries of comedy. Sacha Baron Cohen’s performance as Borat is both fearless and brilliant, making the character unforgettable. The film’s mockumentary style allows for genuine reactions from unsuspecting Americans, resulting in some of the most memorable moments in modern comedy.

While Borat definitely has its controversial moments, its social commentary and clever writing make it a standout film. The movie manages to constantly shock the viewer in the best ways possible.

Trivia

  • Sacha Baron Cohen stayed in character as Borat throughout the entire filming process, even off camera.
  • The film was banned in several countries, including Kazakhstan, upon its release.
  • Many scenes were unscripted and involved real people who were unaware they were being filmed for a comedy.
